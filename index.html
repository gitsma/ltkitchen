Midus

We love a good drink as much as food. Midus is an ancient Lithuanian mead made with honey and berry juice.

Cepelinai

Cepelinai (or didžkukuliai) are among the most important dishes in this Lithuanian food guide.

Kibinai

Kibinai (or kybyn) are traditional Lithuanian pastries associated mostly with the city of Trakai and its ethnic Karaite minority.

Grybukai

Grybukai are Lithuanian cookies. Like kūčiukai, they’re a popular Christmas tradition in Lithuania.

Šaltibarščiai

Šaltibarščiai is a popular summertime soup in Lithuania, known for its bright pink color. It’s a type of cold borscht soup that can be enjoyed as an appetizer or light entree.
